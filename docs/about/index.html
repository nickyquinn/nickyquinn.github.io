About me

I started building websites way back in the glory days of Geocities, Windows 95 and Netscape Navigator, where I learnt how important it was to have a good under construction GIF and a visitor counter. There began a love of building things for the young internet, and a passion for software development.

These days I'm mostly found working with .NET, React and Next.js, building enterprise and consumer facing web applications.
